## Introduction

Welcome to Bubble ("we", "our", or "the app"). Protecting your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the app. By using Bubble, you agree to the data practices described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

- **Account Information**: When you create an account, we may collect your username, email address, and password.
- **User Content**: We store the chat records and content you create in the app so you can access your conversation history.
- **Device Information**: We may collect information about the device you use, including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

### Usage Data

We may also collect information about how you use the app, including:

- Features and pages you access
- Your usage patterns and preferences
- App performance data and error reports

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- Providing, maintaining, and improving our services
- Processing and completing your requests
- Sending service notifications and updates
- Monitoring and analyzing usage trends and preferences
- Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues
- Developing new features and services

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: We may share information with third-party service providers that help us provide our services (such as cloud storage providers).
- **Compliance and Legal Requirements**: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.
- **Business Transfers**: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Your Choices and Rights

You can manage your personal information and privacy preferences through the app settings. You have the right to:

- Access and update your personal information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt out of certain data collection and use
- Request a copy of your personal data

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that we may have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete that information.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new version in the app. We recommend that you review this policy periodically for any changes.
